Real-time speaker diarization & recognition

Powered by SpeechBrain ECAPA-TDNN embeddings, Silero VAD, and faster-whisper.

Workflow:

  1. Enroll two or more speakers — record or upload a few voice samples each (10–30s total per person works best).
  2. Open live transcription — the system streams your mic, detects speaker turns, identifies who is speaking, and labels anyone unenrolled as Unknown Speaker.
